Build a full MVP website for an eldercare education and expert referral platform. 
This is a real business targeting families in crisis, aging individuals, and 
proactive planners navigating eldercare, Medicare/Medicaid, estate planning, 
and probate. The platform serves the NJ/tri-state area initially.

---

## TECH STACK
- React (Vite)
- React Router for navigation
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- No backend required at MVP — use static data and mailto/Calendly links
- Google Fonts for typography
- Fully mobile responsive (users are often on phones in hospitals)

---

## DESIGN DIRECTION
Warm, trustworthy, and calm. This is not a startup or fintech product — 
it's a resource for people under emotional stress. The aesthetic should feel 
like a knowledgeable, compassionate guide.

- Color palette: Deep navy (#1B2A4A) as primary, warm cream/off-white 
  (#FAF7F2) as background, warm gold (#C9922A) as accent, soft sage 
  green (#7A9E7E) as secondary accent
- Typography: Use "Playfair Display" for headings (Google Fonts) — 
  authoritative and warm. Use "Source Serif 4" for body text — readable, 
  human, not clinical
- Avoid: Purple gradients, generic sans-serif, corporate cold aesthetics
- Motion: Subtle fade-in on scroll for content sections. Gentle hover 
  states on cards and buttons. Nothing jarring.
- Visual details: Soft drop shadows on cards, generous whitespace, 
  warm background textures (subtle noise or grain overlay on hero section)

### 2. START HERE PAGE (/start)

A guided 5-question diagnostic flow built as a multi-step form:

Step 1: "What best describes your situation right now?"
- In a crisis and need immediate help
- Managing ongoing care for a loved one
- Planning ahead before anything urgent arises
- I recently lost a loved one and need guidance
- I'm an aging individual planning for myself

Step 2: "Who needs help?"
- An aging parent
- A spouse or partner
- Myself
- Another family member

Step 3: "How urgent is your situation?"
- Extremely urgent — days or less
- Soon — within the next few weeks
- Not urgent — planning ahead

Step 4: "What's already in place?" (multi-select checkboxes)
- [ ] Medicare coverage
- [ ] Medicaid coverage
- [ ] Power of Attorney
- [ ] A will or trust
- [ ] Long-term care insurance
- [ ] None of the above
- [ ] I'm not sure

Step 5: "What concerns you most right now?" (multi-select)
- [ ] Finding and paying for care
- [ ] Understanding Medicare or Medicaid
- [ ] Legal documents and planning
- [ ] What happens after a loved one passes
- [ ] I don't know where to start

Results Page:
- Based on answers, display a personalized "Your Starting Point" card
- Show 2-3 recommended resource sections based on their answers
- Show 1-2 relevant expert types they should connect with
- CTA: "Browse Resources" and "Find an Expert"
- Use simple conditional logic in React state to map answer 
  combinations to recommendations

## CONTENT TONE GUIDELINES
Write all placeholder content in this voice:
- Warm, plain-language, non-clinical
- Empathetic first — acknowledge the difficulty before 
  delivering information
- Never use jargon without immediately explaining it
- Short sentences. Real words. No corporate speak.
- Example: Instead of "Navigate the complexities of eldercare 
  decision-making," write "Figure out what to do when 
  a parent needs help."

## FINAL NOTES
- All forms are frontend-only (no backend). Email capture 
  shows a success state on submit.
- All "Book Consultation" buttons link to https://calendly.com 
  as a placeholder
- Include a floating "Need Help Now?" button fixed to 
  bottom-right corner on all pages that scrolls to 
  email capture or routes to /start
- Ensure WCAG AA accessibility: sufficient color contrast, 
  aria labels on interactive elements, keyboard navigable
- Performance: lazy load images, keep bundle lean
- Include a robots.txt and basic meta tags for SEO on each page
